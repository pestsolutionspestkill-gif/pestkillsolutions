Our Services

Pest Control Services (Jaipur)

Har service me pehle inspection hota hai, phir targeted treatment + safety instructions + follow-up support.

Cockroach Control

Kitchen, sink area, bathroom drains aur dark corners me cockroach activity common hoti hai. Hum gel-baiting + targeted spray/dusting ka combination use karke breeding points break karte hain aur har room me safe coverage dete hain.

Best for: Flats, hostels, restaurants, sweet shops; Treatment includes: harbourage identification, crack & crevice focus, hygiene do’s & don’ts for long lasting results.

Mosquito Control

Mosquitoes breeding mostly stagnant water (cooler tray, pots, drains) se hoti hai. Hum source reduction advice ke saath outdoor fogging/spray aur entry point management (windows/vents area) recommend karte hain.

Best for: Societies, gardens, cafes, schools; Focus: breeding control + adult mosquito reduction for bite pressure kam karna, especially monsoon season me.

Rodent Control (Rats/Mouse)

Rats wiring damage, food contamination aur false ceilings me nesting create kar dete hain. Hum inspection ke basis par bait stations/traps plan karte hain aur entry holes, gaps aur drain routes identify karke proofing guidance dete hain.

Best for: Homes, godowns, shops; Includes: activity mapping, safe placement strategy (kids/pets safety), and follow-up for re-baiting if needed.

Termite Control (Anti-Termite)

Termites silently woodwork, wardrobes aur doors ko damage kar sakte hain. Hum site condition ke hisaab se drilling + injection (spot/area) aur barrier treatment recommend karte hain, taaki colony access cut ho aur future activity reduce ho.

Best for: Wooden furniture, old construction, ground floor units; Includes: inspection of mud tubes, damp areas, and prevention tips (leakage control, storage practices).
