Our Services

Professional Pest Control Services in Jaipur

We provide inspection, treatment and preventive solutions for homes, offices, shops, restaurants and warehouses. For best results, we combine sanitation guidance, proofing (gap sealing), targeted treatments and follow-up visits as required.

Cockroach Control

Cockroaches can contaminate food and kitchen surfaces and may mechanically carry food-borne pathogens, increasing risk of stomach infection/food poisoning-type illness.

Relief with treatment: Gel baiting + crack & crevice treatment reduces hiding spots and breeding, so night sightings, droppings, and bad smell reduce over time (with hygiene guidance).

Termite Treatment

Termites silently damage wooden furniture, doors, wardrobes and sometimes structure—repair cost high ho sakta hai if ignored.

Relief with treatment: Drill & inject / barrier method se termite pathways cut hote hain, woodwork protection improve hoti hai, and future activity risk reduce hota hai (site condition wise).

Anti Termite Piping Treatment

Piping system me fixed treatment points create hote hain jahan se foundation/perimeter area me future anti-termite top-up/re-treatment easily kiya ja sakta hai—especially long-term protection ke liye helpful.

Relief with treatment: Har baar drilling everywhere avoid hota hai; maintenance plan smooth rehta hai, and termite prevention consistent maintain ho sakta hai (site access/design ke according).

Bed Bug Treatment

Bed bugs typically disease spread nahi karte, but bites se itching, sleep disturbance aur kabhi allergic reaction ho sakta hai; scratching se secondary skin infection ka risk bhi hota hai. [web:110]

Relief with treatment: Inspection + targeted treatment + washing/heat-dry guidance se bite cycle break hota hai, better sleep aati hai aur itching episodes reduce hote hain. [web:110]

Mosquito Control

Mosquitoes dengue jaise diseases spread karte hain; breeding control (standing water removal/covering) is a key prevention step. [web:108]

Relief with treatment: Source reduction + larval control + fogging/spray (as required) se bite pressure aur indoor activity kam hoti hai, especially monsoon months me. [web:108]

Rodent Control (Rat/Mouse)

Rodents food contamination, foul smell, false ceiling me noise, aur wiring damage (short-circuit/fire risk) create kar sakte hain.

Relief with treatment: Traps/bait stations + entry point proofing guidance se movement routes break hote hain, activity reduce hoti hai, and repeated sightings kam hoti hain.

Ant Control

Ant trails kitchen me food contamination aur nuisance create karte hain—especially sweets, atta, and pet food ke around.

Relief with treatment: Targeted baiting/trail treatment se colony feeding cycle break hota hai; sealing + hygiene tips se re-entry reduce hota hai.

Spider Control

Spiders webs corners/ceilings me hygiene issue create karte hain and many people feel anxiety/fear, especially store rooms and balconies.

Relief with treatment: Web removal + corner treatment + preventive cleaning guidance se webs and sightings reduce hote hain.

Bird Netting Service

Bird netting balconies, shafts, terraces aur open areas me birds ko enter/roost karne se block karta hai, jisse droppings, feathers, smell aur mess kam hota hai.

Relief with treatment: Long-term exclusion milta hai; cleaning frequency kam hoti hai aur droppings disturb hone se banne wali dust exposure risk bhi reduce hoti hai. [web:123]

Bird Spikes Installation

Bird spikes AC ledges, parapet, signboards aur window sills par install hote hain—ye narrow ledges par birds ko land/roost karne se deter karte hain.

Relief with treatment: Roosting aur nesting rukti hai, droppings/smell/blocked drains ke complaints kam hote hain; netting ke saath combine karke best results milte hain. [web:132]

Commercial Pest Management

Commercial sites (restaurants/clinics/warehouses) me pests customer complaints, hygiene risk, and compliance/audit issues create karte hain.

Relief with treatment: Customized plan + monitoring + periodic visits se infestation control better hota hai and recurrence risk reduce hota hai.
